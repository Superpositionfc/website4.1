About Us

Superposition is an international non-profit bridging the gender gap in STEM. Our mission is to empower women in STEM by creating educational opportunities & supportive spaces for developing their passions.

Since 2016, we've held the Bay Area's largest all-women and non-binary hackathon in partnership with companies like VSCO and Uber. Starting in March 2020, we expanded beyond a 24-hour hackathon to provide year-round opportunities for our community through a webinar series, Uber mentorship program, chapter program, and more. With our 34 chapters, events and content, we've reached 300,000+ individuals in 44 states and 52 countries.

Get involved with Superposition by joining our team or founding your own chapter! Find all of our latest Superposition opportunities here.

Female empowerment

does not stop after 24 hours, and neither should Superposition. In addition to hosting an annual hackathon, we're expanding our initiative to provide year-round opportunities to our community.
