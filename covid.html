SUPPORTING STUDENTS & HEALTHCARE WORKERS



Our Initiative

During the pandemic, Superposition is aiding both students and healthcare workers. We are hosting a free virtual workshop & panel series for students of all ages and gender identities to help our community students continue their education in technical & practical skills. So far, we have hosted workshops like High School Programs & Internships, Professional Networking, and Intro to Python. We've also hosted a panel in collaboration with Girls Talk Tech featuring the organizers of the largest all-women and non-binary hackathons from around the country: Technica, TechTogether Boston, Flawless Hacks, and Superposition.
