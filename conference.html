OPPORTUNITIES

Become an Ambassador

INFO APPLY

EmpowHERment Ambassadors will help share our conferences with young women & non-binary folks around the world! Ambassadors will access exclusive leadership workshops, earn Presidential Volunteer Service Award hours, be eligible for a $100 scholarship + raffle prizes, and more! Successful ambassadors will also be invited to join the Superposition Public Relations team. Apply by January 10 at 11:59 PM PST.

Scholarships

APPLY

We will be offering scholarships to support women in Social Impact & Education. Each category will have 3 prizes: $250, $150, $100. Application information will be released soon. Applicants may apply to more than one category. We’re also offering an exclusive $100 scholarship available only to Superposition ambassadors of any gender identity. Applications have closed.

FAQS

Our event & prizes are 100% free.

Our EmpowHERment Conference is open to everyone, regardless of age and gender identity.

The conference will be online!

The event is scheduled to be from 10 AM PST - 4 PM PST on January 16th, 2021. Check out the schedule above for more details about specific workshops!

While it’s recommended to attend most of the conference, we do not require attending all workshops to win prizes.

Our Social Impact and Education Scholarships are available to female-identifying and non-binary individuals. However, our Education Scholarship is open only to students enrolled in any educational institution. The Ambassador Scholarship is available only to Superposition ambassadors.
